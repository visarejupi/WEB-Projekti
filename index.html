3 IN 1 TREE LAMP


A unique and
compact design


This 3 IN 1 TREE LAMP features a compact design structure, a
Bluetooth speaker, a wireless charger, and of course the lamp.
The entire assembly is in light of tree design which is super
aesthetic to look at. Besides, this lamp has Stepless dimming
lights through its wood shade which is so cool to operate.

BLUETOOTH SPEAKER

Just pair your device to the lamp, and play your
favourite music wirelessly.

SLEEP MODE

It features a "Sleep Mode" setting that turns the
light off automatically after 30 minutes so that you
can sleep soundly and comfortably.

MORE THAN JUST A MERE lamp

It's sleek and exhibits a thin, cherry wood shade,
and brightness that can be smoothly adjusted via
touch controls on the base.
